About Me

I am a plant lover, "I have had a love for plants since childhood. In 2019, I started a small
Garden in my hom, and later collected information about various plants through online media
and social media.I learned to cultivate them. Today, I am a hobbyist who takes care of and grows
more than 100 plants.I am proud to provide Plant Care knowledge to many."I have about a hundred
varieties of plants.Cacti, succulents, indoor plants Caladium, and besides these, I also have
water plants such as lotus and water lily varieties.I have an online sale. I send plants online
through courier. I have a service within and outside Kerala.After removing the pot and
potting mix,the roots of the plant are well covered and neatly packed and sent by courier.
I collect all my plants online and propagate them from them.I give my plants only organic
fertilizers. I do not use chemical fertilizers.
